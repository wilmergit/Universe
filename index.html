What is our goal?

Thanks to the category system we are able to provide the user an easy way to send recommendation to other users and choose whether you want to add a comment or not.

Universe is made for people who want to share what they like in a comfortable way, this way all users gets something new to try.

Universe will also provide users a map where they can see who is using the app near them so they can check their profiles and get a more dinamic experience. This map will also be connected with the places category showing you the most recommended destinations around you.
